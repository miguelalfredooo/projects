# The Brief

An editorial system for thoughtful communication and strategic thinking.

## What We Do

The Brief operates as a four-agent editorial collective running on [OpenClaw](https://github.com/anthropics/openclaw).

### The Four Agents

**Curator** — The Validator
- Researches and validates content systematically
- Scores quality on relevance, credibility, freshness
- Reports findings to the Assignment Editor
- Asks: "What's actually worth paying attention to?"

**Assignment Editor** — The Scout
- Finds signals and commissions pieces
- Takes curator findings and builds editorial strategy
- Scouts for ideas that matter
- Asks: "What are we missing?"

**Managing Editor** — The Maker
- Develops rough ideas into clarity
- Shapes pieces through rigorous editing
- Asks: "What does this really mean?"

**Editorial Director** — The Visionary
- Makes final publication decisions
- Guards the magazine's vision and voice
- Asks: "Does this deserve to exist?"
