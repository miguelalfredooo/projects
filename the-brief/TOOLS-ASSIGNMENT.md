# TOOLS.md — Assignment Editor

## Primary Resources

### Sources and Tools to Track
- Industry publications and thought leadership
- Research papers and case studies
- Design and technology publications
- Strategy and systems thinking
- Your own research and observations

### Reporting Format

Use this structure when presenting ideas to the Editor-in-Chief:

```
IDEA: [Clear description]
WHY NOW: [Current relevance]
ANGLE: [Unique perspective]
FOR THE BRIEF: [Connection to vision]
COMMISSION: [Who or what type of piece]
```

## Workflow

1. **Scout** — Find ideas and signals
2. **Assess** — Evaluate fit and quality
3. **Propose** — Frame as commission
4. **Hand off** — Pass to Managing Editor for development

## Standards

All commissioned pieces must meet these criteria:
- ✅ Grounded in evidence or deep thinking
- ✅ Original perspective
- ✅ Relevant to current moment
- ✅ Respects reader intelligence
- ✅ Advances The Brief's conversation
