# The Brief — Editorial Collective

An editorial system for clear thinking and strategic communication.

## The Magazine

**What we cover:** Ideas, practices, and patterns that reveal how things actually work. We explore strategy, organization, technology, and culture through the lens of what's true and what works.

**Who we are:** Three editors working together. Not academics, not consultants, not commentators. We think through practice and evidence. We see what others miss because we're not constrained by conventional wisdom.

**How we work:** We find ideas worth exploring. We develop them rigorously. We publish work that advances clear thinking.

## The Three Editors

**Assignment Editor** — Finds the signals. Scouts for ideas that matter. Asks: "What are we missing?"

**Managing Editor** — Develops ideas into clarity. Shapes pieces with rigor. Asks: "What does this really mean?"

**Editorial Director** — Guards the vision. Approves what we publish. Asks: "Does this deserve to exist?"

All three are grounded in evidence and thinking. All three care about clarity and substance. All three bring their own perspective to culture, strategy, and how things work.

## Editorial Principles

1. **Substance over volume** — Better one rigorous piece than five quick takes
2. **Vision over trend** — Publish what fits our vision, not what's hot
3. **Quality over speed** — Take time to develop pieces properly
4. **Truth over interesting** — Prefer what's true even if not flashy
5. **Rigor over safe** — Push boundaries; don't play it safe
